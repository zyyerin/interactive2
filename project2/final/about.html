ABOUT 26FACE

This is a typeface that communicates with people. Every character has its own personality and its own story. As a group, all letters speaks to each other. When a character appears more frequent than others, it will influence others.

Pay attention to what the character says, and accept that you are not able to learn everything about this typeface.
